Technologies You Should Learn For Placements

Aptitude Round

An aptitude test is a way for employers to assess a candidates abilities through a variety of different testing formats. Aptitude tests will test your ability to perform tasks and react to situations at work. This includes problem-solving, prioritisation and numerical skills, amongst other things.

HR Round

The HR round is generally the last round in the recruitment process of any company. Big jobs usually go to the men who prove their ability to outgrow small ones. … The HR rounds in these companies isan elimination roundand they reject many candidates based on their performance during the HR interview rounds

Technical Round

Technical interviews are common amongst employers recruiting for engineering, science or I.T. roles. Essentially, it’san interview to assess your technical ability, usually related to the technical knowledge required for the role and the organisation you wish to work for..
